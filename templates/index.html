How to use this ?

In the search bar, there are three fields that compose a citation:

  • the journal name: suggestions (based on what you selected on the left) appear as you are typing,
  • the volume,
  • the (starting page).

Then, you can select what you want:

  • an url: fastest option, but not guaranteed to be correct,
  • a DOI: slower option, but guaranteed to be correct (checked against the journal).

Click the "GET" button and the result appears below.
